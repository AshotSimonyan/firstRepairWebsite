Why Choose Cape Cod Appliance Service?

We provide expert repair services for refrigerators, dishwashers, washers, dryers, built-in microwave ovens, ranges, wall ovens and vent hoods, ice makers, wine coolers and trash compactors. We offer free estimates for dryer vent cleaning and replacement services. Cape Cod Appliance Service specializes in high end appliance repair. Our outstanding services were recognized with the 2015 Bosch Servicer of the Year Award. We are committed to being environmentally friendly and our technicians drive late model flex fuel vans and recycle all scrap metal and packaging from parts and appliances.

Service at Cape Cod Appliance Service
Is Unmatched

Your phone call to Cape Cod Appliance will be answered personally; you will never be placed in a computerized phone tree or sent to voicemail. We have a 24-hour answering service for any emergency. When your Bosch, Sub Zero or other high-end appliance is broken, our customer service reps will schedule a fast and efficient service call with our factory trained technicians. We will swiftly resolve your appliance issues with same day service for all makes including Sub Zero repair. Our easily recognized Cape Cod Appliance service vans are stocked with factory certified parts and we try to repair and restore your appliance with a single service call. All of our work and parts are guaranteed.
